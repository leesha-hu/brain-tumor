Welcome to TumorVision

TumorVision is an AI-powered platform designed to classify brain tumors using MRI images. It supports multi-class diagnosis, including Glioma, Meningioma, Pituitary, and No Tumor.

The system uses advanced deep learning models such as ResNet50, Xception, DenseNet121, and EfficientNetB4 to ensure high accuracy and robust performance.

TumorVision integrates Explainable AI (XAI) techniques like Grad-CAM and SHAP to visually highlight important regions in MRI scans, making predictions interpretable for doctors, students, and researchers.
