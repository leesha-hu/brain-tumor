Brain Health Tips
Back to Dashboard
  • Engage in regular physical exercise such as walking, yoga, or light workouts to improve blood flow to the brain.
  • Include brain-healthy foods like leafy greens, fruits, nuts, fish, and whole grains in your daily diet.
  • Stay mentally active by reading, solving puzzles, or learning new skills.
  • Ensure adequate sleep every night, as sleep is essential for brain recovery and function.
  • Manage stress through meditation, deep breathing, or relaxation techniques.
  • Avoid smoking and limit alcohol consumption to protect brain health.
Note: Contact doctors for more information.
